## Description

Beat Ya Feet is a street dance form that originated in Washington D.C. in the early 1990s. It is characterized by its fast-paced footwork and energetic movements, often performed to go-go music.

## Music

Go-go music, a subgenre of funk that originated in D.C., featuring live bands and a strong emphasis on percussion.
