## Description

House dance emerged in the underground club scene of Chicago and New York in the late 1970s and early 1980s. It's known for its fast, intricate footwork and fluid body movements.

## Music

House music with its four-on-the-floor beats, typically 120-130 BPM. Influenced by disco, funk, and electronic music from Chicago and New York clubs.

## Notable Figures

- Ejoe Wilson - Pioneer of house dance, known for innovative footwork
- Brian 'Footwork' Green - Legendary house dancer and teacher
- Caleaf Sellers - Influential house dancer and choreographer
