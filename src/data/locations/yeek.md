## Description

Yeek is a high-energy dance form that emerged from Atlanta's strip club and urban dance scene in the 2010s. It features rapid twerking movements, knee bounces, and synchronized group performances often seen in music videos.

## Music

Atlanta trap and hip-hop music with heavy bass and rapid hi-hat patterns. The style complements the bounce and energy of Atlanta's signature sound from producers like Metro Boomin and Zaytoven.

## Notable Figures

- Various Atlanta strip club dancers who pioneered the style
- Social media influencers who popularized Yeek
- Atlanta's dance community and choreographers
