## Description

Baltimore Club is a high-energy dance form that emerged from Baltimore's underground club scene in the late 1980s. It features rapid footwork, energetic movements, and is performed to the fast-paced Baltimore Club music genre.

## Music

Baltimore Club music features breakbeats, samples, and tempos around 130 BPM. The sound influenced Jersey Club and other regional styles. Key producers include Rod Lee, KW Griff, and DJ Technics.

## Notable Figures

- Rod Lee - Pioneer of Baltimore Club music
- KW Griff - Influential Baltimore Club producer
- DJ Technics - Key figure in developing the Baltimore sound
