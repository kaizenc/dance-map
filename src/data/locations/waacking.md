## Description

Waacking is a dance form that originated in the LGBTQ+ clubs of Los Angeles in the 1970s. It's characterized by its expressive arm movements and poses, often performed to disco music.

## Music

Disco music, particularly the tracks that were popular in the 1970s club scene.

- Toni Basil - Choreographer and member of The Lockers
- Fred Berry - Member of The Lockers, known as 'Rerun'
