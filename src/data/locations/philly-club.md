## Description

Philly Club is Philadelphia's answer to Baltimore and Jersey Club, featuring fast-paced footwork and energetic movements. The style reflects Philadelphia's unique urban dance culture and street style.

## Music

Philly Club music takes influence from Baltimore Club, featuring fast tempos, breakbeats, and Philadelphia's distinctive hip-hop influence. The genre emphasizes heavy bass and rapid-fire percussion.
