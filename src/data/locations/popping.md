## Description

Popping is a funk dance form that originated in Fresno and Los Angeles in the late 1970s. It's characterized by sudden muscle contractions that create a 'popping' or 'hitting' effect, combined with fluid movements and mime-like isolations.

## Music

Funk and electro-funk music, particularly tracks with strong rhythmic patterns. The Electric Boogaloos popularized the style to music by artists like Kraftwerk and early hip-hop beats.

## Notable Figures

- Boogaloo Sam - Creator of popping, founder of Electric Boogaloos
- Popin' Pete - Member of Electric Boogaloos, popularized the style worldwide
- Skeeter Rabbit - Electric Boogaloos member, innovator of popping techniques
