## Description

Jersey Club is a high-energy dance music and dance form that emerged from Newark, New Jersey in the early 2000s. It's characterized by rapid bed squeaks, vocal chops, and aggressive, fast-paced movements.

## Music

Jersey Club music features fast tempos (typically 130-140 BPM), heavy use of the 'bed squeak' sample, vocal chops, and Baltimore Club influences. The genre became mainstream in the 2010s.

- DJ Sliink - Pioneer of Jersey Club sound
- R3ll - Influential Jersey Club producer
- Uniiqu3 - Jersey Club DJ and producer who brought the sound to global audiences
